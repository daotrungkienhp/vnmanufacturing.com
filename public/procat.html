OEM/ODM, FOB
Production

With 92 years of textile expertise, Thygesen Textile is your trusted partner for global excellence.


We are proud to be one of a few textile manufacturers in Vietnam specializing in OEM, FOB production, transforming your unique ideas into reality with full customization options such as fabric sourcing/ development, custom dyeing, logo printing/ embroidery, labeling, hang tag and packaging.


With a keen focus on both woven and knit apparel, our versatility spans the spectrum from sporty activewear to chic fashionwear and comfortable underwear. 


Our team simplifies your fashion journey! No tech pack? No problem. We have a technical team to assist you in creating technical documents. We also provide ODM collection for you to choose from our curated styles and customize them to reflect your brand’s identity.

CMPT By Experienced
Sewing Workers

At Thygesen, besides full OEM/ODM service, we also offer CMPT (Cutting, Making, Packing, and Thread). Precision in cutting, skilled craftsmanship in making, and meticulous attention to packing ensure the highest quality garments.


In the seemingly small details, like the thread, our skilled workers contribute to the durability and aesthetics of the final product. Operating under the stringent standards of WRAP/SA8000 certification, we emphasize social and environmental responsibility throughout the production process.
